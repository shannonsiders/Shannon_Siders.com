About

Hi, I'm Shannon Siders! I have three years of full-time experience in the sports communications and marketing industry, with countless other intern and volunteer experiences. In addition to my day job, I am an Ambassador for Family & Children's Place, a Code Louisville student and a member of the Young Professional's Association of Louisville. As a proud graduate of both Bellarmine University (BA Communication and Political Science, May 2010) and the University of Louisville (MS Sport Administration, December 2011), I also enjoy staying involved with those institutions.

If you'd like to follow me on Twitter, my username is @shannonsiders.
